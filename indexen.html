ABOUT

  • Dear Visitor!

  • Animals have been around me since I was little, I have always been interested in them, I love to spend time with them. My first puppy was Betti, who I found when I was a kid and with whom I learned this beautiful profession from my friend Esther, owner of the Dog Kartel Dog Salon, with whom I still work. At the moment two little beauties Rübe dog and Cakk kitten make my days delightful.

POLICY

  • The health of puppies and kittens is important to us, so we do not accept pets with flea, sick or in heat. However, we will be happy to give you advice, tips for flea removal, and a veterinarian who can help you and your pet.

  • Choosing a date for cat cosmetics sometimes takes a little more time because you have to choose the time when no dog is in the shop. We cannot accept aggressive cats.

  • Problematic doggie cosmetics (snapper, very frightened) are also possible, in which case the work is likely to take longer, so you should expect extra costs. Unfortunately, there are extreme cases that we are not able to handle, but we can offer you the help of a trainer.

Let's help shelter dogs!

  • Ant Community has been supporting poor rural shelters, organizations and animal welfare for years.

  • Alliance for Animals Foundation's purpose is the protection, reception, health care, neutering, identification, and return to an appropriate owner of orphaned or rescued animals from the shelters or thrown, abused, tortured by their owners.

  • We know that donations to puppies and kittens are often difficult to deliver. Therefore, we thought our cosmetics could be a collection point in Budapest, so we would take the donations to the Mátészalka Shelter.

  • Things they might need: nutrition, canned food, towels, disinfectants and cleaners, anthelmintics, probiotics (such as Protexin Pro-choline paste), bowls, leashes and collars.

  • We are closed on Mondays. Before you come, write a message or call us to make sure we are here :)

  • Thanks in advance for the little orphans!
